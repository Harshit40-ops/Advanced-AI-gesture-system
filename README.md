AI Gesture Control System 🖐️✨

A real-time, webcam-based hand-gesture system that lets you control your PC and trigger cinematic AR effects — all with your hands. Built in Python with OpenCV + MediaPipe (Tasks API), runs fully offline on a normal laptop webcam.

🎮 Control

Volume / mute, media play-pause & track control
EMA-smoothed mouse: move, left/right click
Modes (media · mouse · presentation) switched by gesture
10 static gestures with stable N-frame confirmation + cooldown
🎬 Cinematic AR effects

🔮 Nuclear reactor core — open both palms (fiery iris core, shockwaves, orbital trails, containment field)
🔱 Sudarshan Chakra — point your index finger (flaming spinning discus, charge & throw)
⚡🔥❄️ Elemental powers — fist = fire, peace = ice, rock = lightning
🌌 Sci-fi background — replace your room with a synthwave world (b)
🌈 Fingertip trails + 💥 energy blast (force-push)
🔊 Procedural sound FX + 🗣️ spoken voice call-outs
🛠️ Tech: Python · OpenCV · MediaPipe (Hand / Selfie-Segmentation) · NumPy · pycaw · pyautogui · sounddevice
